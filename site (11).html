What Is Jova Chain?

Jova Chain is a next-generation Ethereum Layer 2 blockchain designed to give creators, brands, and communities full control over their identity, content, and money.

With ultra-low fees, lightning-fast transactions, and FTMX as the native gas token, Jova Chain is secure, scalable, and fully EVM-compatible.
